About Jose

Jose Perez is a director and visual storyteller based in Valencia, CA. He specializes in cinematic brand films and narrative content that cuts through the noise and moves audiences to act.

As the founder of Perez & Co. Films, Jose leads a production company built on one belief: every brand has a story worth telling right. His work spans commercial campaigns, documentary, and high-stakes brand launches.
